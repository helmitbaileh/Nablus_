What is Yasmeenah Alley

Yasmeenah Alley in the city of Nablus is one of the oldest neighborhoods, characterized by its narrow streets and traditional stone houses. The narrow alleys stretch between the old houses, reflecting the city's history and culture. Its name reflects the beauty of jasmine that adorns the walls and fences, giving it a special charm and a fragrant scent that fills the air. The alley is known for its traditional spirit, and its residents gather with a spirit of affection and camaraderie, making it a destination for visitors seeking historical beauty and authentic popular atmosphere in the heart of the ancient city of Nablus.
