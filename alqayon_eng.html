What is Al-Qariyun Alley

Al-Qariyun Alley in Nablus is one of the historical neighborhoods with an Ottoman urban heritage. It includes an ancient mosque, a traditional market, and craftsmen working in traditional crafts. Tourists are attracted to explore its beauty, traditional spirit, and rich culture.
