The Old City

What is the Old City

The Old City of Nablus is one of the main tourist destinations in Palestine, known for its charming views and narrow stone-paved streets dating back to ancient times. The Old City houses numerous historical and cultural landmarks such as the Mosque of Prophet Yunus and the ancient Qal'at al-Qahira (Cairo Castle), along with its traditional markets reflecting the bustling commercial life of the city. The Old City offers a unique tourist experience blending history and culture, where visitors can enjoy strolling through its narrow streets, visiting museums and art exhibitions, as well as tasting traditional cuisine in restaurants and cafes. Nablus' Old City is a must-visit destination for those interested in exploring the rich Palestinian heritage and experiencing an atmosphere of authenticity and beauty.
