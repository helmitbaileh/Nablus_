Welcome to Habla Alley

What is Habla Alley

It is one of the neighborhoods of the old town in Nablus, located in its northeastern part. Its streets are built on the rope road, meaning a group of stones supporting each other to form the wall in a spontaneous way. Habla Alley contains several archaeological landmarks such as the shrine of Sheikh Musallam and the Prophets Mosque, as well as the Agha Al-Namr Palace. Several alleys branch off from it, such as Al-Fiqous and Sheikh Musallam.
