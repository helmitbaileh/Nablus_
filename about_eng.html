What is our Streets, Our Stories Initiative?

The initiative was launched by students and academics at An-Najah National University in collaboration with the Municipality of Nablus, aiming to enhance and develop the aesthetics of our wonderful city. The idea of the initiative relies on placing barcode symbols in various areas of Nablus. By scanning these symbols, you will be directly transferred to a pre-programmed website containing comprehensive information about the location in both Arabic and English. The initiative aims to assist both visitors and local residents in exploring our city with ease, providing essential information about tourist and cultural sites, and even guiding them on the optimal routes to reach their destinations smoothly and efficiently. This initiative reflects our commitment to promoting tourism and raising awareness about our cultural heritage, reaffirming our continuous development and innovation in our beautiful city.
