What is Bab Khan Al-Tujjar

Khan Al-Tujjar in Nablus is an important historical and commercial center that retains its charm throughout the ages. The Khan embodies an ancient Ottoman legacy and includes a collection of shops and traditional markets that reflect the activity of trade and traditional crafts. Ottoman inscriptions surround its walls, expressing the heritage of the region. The Khan is considered a tourist and cultural attraction for visitors who wish to explore the history and heritage of Nablus.
