Welcome to Al-Qaysareyah Alley

What is Al-Qaysareyah Alley

It is one of the neighborhoods of the old town in Nablus, occupying the southeast corner of it. It was named "Qaysareyah" after the Roman Caesar. It contains many ancient architectural art remains, including the archaeological site beneath the school of Zafir El-Masri, which dates back to the Roman era. Below it is a tunnel containing a water channel from the same historical period. It includes Al-Jabaliyah, Al-Jawza, and Bir Al-Dulab alleys.
